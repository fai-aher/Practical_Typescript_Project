Breaking Bad

Breaking Bad is an American television series created by Vince Gilligan. The show aired on AMC from 2008 to 2013, consisting of five seasons. It tells the story of Walter White, a struggling and depressed high school chemistry teacher who is diagnosed with lung cancer.
